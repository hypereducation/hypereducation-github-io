# HyperEducation

HyperEducation is a non-profit startup founded in Pakistan, on a mission to help communities across the world access data skills and careers they deserve — at zero cost to learners.

We exist because the data profession has an access problem. Entry-level roles increasingly require certifications, portfolio projects, and experience with tools like Microsoft Fabric and Power BI — prerequisites that are difficult to acquire without an existing professional network or the means to pay for training. For talented people from underprivileged backgrounds, that gap can feel impossible to cross alone.

HyperEducation is being built to bridge it.

## What we offer

- **Power BI for Analysts** — a 10-week structured track taking learners from spreadsheet user to job-ready analyst, covering data modelling, DAX, and report design
- **Fabric Fundamentals** — an introduction to the Microsoft Fabric ecosystem: Lakehouses, pipelines, and the OneLake architecture
- **Data Career Launchpad** — a combined technical and career track including portfolio projects, mock interviews, and 1:1 mentorship for learners re-entering the workforce or overcoming long-term unemployment

All programmes are delivered at no cost to the learner.

## Who we are built for

- People without a university degree who have the aptitude but not the credentials
- Recent immigrants and refugees whose existing analytical skills are invisible to local job markets
- Single parents and care leavers who need flexible, human-centred learning
- The long-term unemployed who need more than a course — they need a portfolio, a mentor, and someone in their corner

## Status
